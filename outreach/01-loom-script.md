## Recording tips

1. **Stand, don't sit.** Voice carries better.
2. **First take is rarely best — record 3, pick the steadiest.**
3. **Speak ~15% slower than natural.** Saudi business culture rewards measured cadence.
4. **Look at camera, not screen, during face-cam moments.**
5. **Don't apologize.** No "I hope you don't mind". You're a peer.
6. **Pause for half a second after each section.** Lets the viewer absorb.
