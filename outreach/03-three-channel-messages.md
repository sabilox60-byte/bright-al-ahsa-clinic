## Why this sequence wins

1. **Three channels same morning** = he sees you in his important inbox AND his LinkedIn AND his clinic's WhatsApp. Cross-channel confirmation = you're real.

2. **Working demo in every message** = he can verify your skill in 30 seconds. No pitch deck. No "we will build". The build exists.

3. **No-chase rule** = exactly opposite of every other agency contacting him. Saudi business culture rewards restraint. Vendors who chase are losing; vendors who deliver and wait are winning.

4. **Closing offer is non-binding** = "keep the demo as a benchmark even if we don't work together" — psychologically impossible to be hostile to.

5. **Day-9 final note frames you as the high-class loser** = if he never replies, you exit graciously, and 6 months from now when he's frustrated with his current agency, your demo is what he remembers.
