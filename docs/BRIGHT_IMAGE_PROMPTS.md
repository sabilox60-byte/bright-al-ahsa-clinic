# Bright Specialized Clinics — Image Prompts (Persuasion-First, v2)

> **Run in:** Nano Banana Pro · [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)
> **Strategic intent:** every image must trigger one feeling in the visitor —
> *"I want to be her, leaving that clinic right now."*
> **Constraint:** PDPL + SFDA prohibit identifiable patient faces.
> **Solution:** show the WOMAN, show the MOMENT, but never the recognizable face.
> Mirror-neuron triggers > stock-luxe abstraction. **Every time.**

---

## ⓪ THE PSYCHOLOGY (read before generating)

Most clinic websites show:
- Empty rooms (no human → no identification)
- Abstract serums + brass instruments (lux but unrelatable)
- Stock smiling models (fake → repels Saudi female buyers)

**These all fail the persuasion test.** A real Saudi woman browsing on her phone at 11 PM does not think "I want to be near that brass instrument." She thinks "could that be me?"

**Bright's images must trigger 4 reactions in sequence (within 3 seconds):**

1. **Identification** — "She's like me — Saudi, modern, my age, my style"
2. **Recognition** — "I know this moment. The exact second after a procedure where you check the mirror"
3. **Aspiration** — "She looks satisfied. I want that"
4. **Action urgency** — "I should book. Now. Before this feeling expires"

We achieve this by:
- Showing **real-looking modern Saudi women** (NOT models, NOT actors)
- Capturing **the specific 30-second moment** right after a procedure when satisfaction is peaking
- Composing so the **face is never identifiable** (back-of-head, partial profile, hands at face, mirror reflection from angle, sunglasses, hijab/abaya hood)
- Using **natural daylight** (not "golden hour cinematic") — feels real, not staged
- Adding **modern Saudi context cues** (abaya texture, smartphone in hand, branded perfume bottle, designer sunglasses on table, valet ticket, expensive car key on counter)

**The clinic owner who sees this site should feel one thing:** *every woman who lands here wants to book — and I'm not getting any of them.*

## ① CRITICAL STYLE LOCK — paste at the start of every Gemini conversation

```
STYLE LOCK FOR BRIGHT SPECIALIZED CLINICS

We are creating photographs that will appear on a Saudi premium
medical clinic website. The audience is Saudi women aged 28-50,
upper-middle to wealthy, sophisticated, busy. They scroll on their
iPhones.

PHOTOGRAPHIC DISCIPLINE:
- Natural documentary photograph — NOT cinematic, NOT a movie still,
  NOT dramatic lighting. Real. Candid-feeling. Unposed.
- Modern Saudi woman (clearly Arab/Khaleeji features), 30-45 years
  old, NOT a model. Wearing contemporary modest fashion: black or
  cream abaya, sometimes with hood up; OR designer modest casual
  (loose silk shirt, wide trousers); sometimes hijab.
- Natural daylight from a window or interior daylight bulb. Avoid
  golden-hour glow unless specified. We want REAL evening, REAL
  morning, REAL midday — not magazine cover light.
- Subject occupies 60-70% of frame in the satisfaction moments
  (close enough to feel her, far enough to never reveal full face).
- Slight handheld feel — minor depth-of-field, occasional natural
  motion blur. Smartphone-photograph-feeling but with full-frame
  camera depth.

HARD COMPLIANCE — NEVER include:
- Any recognizable face. Full-face photos are forbidden by Saudi
  PDPL + SFDA for our use case.
- Patient before/after comparisons (regulated)
- Synthetic or cinematic over-saturation
- "Movie still" / "epic" / "dramatic" composition
- Models in poses — they must look like a real woman caught in a
  real moment
- Identifying surroundings (no specific hotel logos, no specific
  car brands visible)

ALLOWED for face handling (any of these):
- Back of head with elegant hair / hijab
- Partial face — frame cut at the nose or below
- Profile silhouette against window light
- Face reflected in mirror but soft-focus / out of focus
- Hands at face, fingers covering mouth (a quiet satisfied gesture)
- Oversized designer sunglasses
- Hood of abaya pulled forward

BRAND VISUAL LANGUAGE:
- Warm cream interiors (#f8f6f0) with brass detail
- Soft purple-violet ambient at frame edges (Bright's night-glow)
- Modern Saudi luxury — Diriyah-influenced, not Dubai-flashy
- Restraint > maximalism. ONE focal point per photo.

OUTPUT: 4K, 16:9 unless specified. RAW photograph quality.
PNG with transparency or high-quality JPG.
```

## ⑧ ANTI-PROMPT NOTES (avoid these traps with Nano Banana Pro)

Nano Banana Pro tends toward cinematic. To force REAL feel:

| If image comes out... | Add this to next prompt |
|---|---|
| Cinematic / movie-still | "candid documentary photograph, natural light only, NOT cinematic" |
| Model in pose | "real woman caught mid-gesture, unposed, smartphone-photograph style" |
| Face too visible | "her face is hidden by [hair / mirror reflection / phone / sunglasses / hood]" |
| Magazine perfect | "slightly imperfect composition, real lived-in environment, NOT styled set" |
| Sterile / fake | "real home interior with personal details visible — keys, glasses, half-empty cup" |
| Too dramatic | "honest natural daylight, no golden hour, no theatrical lighting" |
| Patient face simulation | "no patient face visible at any angle, recompose with face out of frame" |

## ⑨ The persuasion check (for every image before you save it)

Hold the image up and ask:

1. **Identification:** Does a Saudi woman aged 30-45 think "she's like me"?
2. **Recognition:** Is the moment she captures a moment a real woman would have?
3. **Aspiration:** Does the satisfaction feel earned, not staged?
4. **Action:** Does the visitor feel "I want this — when can I book"?

If 4/4 → save it.
If 3/4 → regenerate with anti-prompts above.
If 2/4 or below → reject and rewrite.
