# SKILLS PLAYBOOK — The Honeys We Offer Alongside the Website

> **What this is:** The supplementary services ("honeys") we sell to clinics in addition to (or instead of) the website. Each is a self-contained skill with its own playbook.
>
> **Why we have them:** The website is a long-term asset. But clinics need IMMEDIATE measurable impact to trust us. The honeys deliver that proof, then the website + retainer follow.

---

## The 6 honeys

### 1. The Eid Acceleration Pack — SAR 25-45K · 14 days

The default upsell after Honey Drop deployment. Once a clinic sees Day 1-3 impact from our free creatives, this is the natural next ask.

**Deliverables (14-day sprint):**
- 14 daily Snapchat reels (Khaleeji, doctor-voiced)
- 3 micro-influencer collabs (free-treatment-for-post)
- 1 WhatsApp 11-min SLA responder (n8n + Claude)
- Daily booking dashboard
- Day 14 attribution report

**When to sell:** Clinic just tested a Honey Drop and saw impact.

**Playbook file:** `docs/skills/01-eid-acceleration.md` (to be built per-clinic)

### 2. The 90-Day Sprint — SAR 75K + 15% rev share · 90 days

The full Sprint. Major commitment. This is the conversion target after the Eid Acceleration Pack performs.

**Deliverables:**
- Everything in #1, scaled 6× duration
- Full website deployment (this template, fully filled)
- Trust Vault page live

**When to sell:** After Eid Acceleration delivered ≥20% booking lift.

### 3. The 12-Month Occasion Engine — SAR 65K/month · ongoing

The retainer. The clinic stays with us indefinitely. We run every Saudi occasion (14 windows/year) as a booking peak.

**Deliverables:**
- All Sprint deliverables, ongoing
- Saudi Calendar Engine auto-activates per occasion
- Quarterly creative refresh (12 new doctor reels per quarter)
- Monthly performance review with clinic owner
- Clinic-specific A/B testing per occasion
- Strategic advisory (1 hour/month with founder)

**When to sell:** After 90-Day Sprint converted ≥1 owner-named outcome (e.g., "filled Ramadan slots by 80%").

**When to sell:** Clinic owner panicking 1-2 weeks before an occasion (Eid, National Day) about empty slots.

**When to sell:** Clinic owner mentions "my agency runs everything and I don't know what they're publishing." Easy proof of expertise.

## What's NOT in the playbook (intentionally)

- ❌ **One-off social media posts** — we don't sell single deliverables. Too cheap, too commoditized.
- ❌ **Lead generation only** — leads without conversion = client thinks we're a vendor, not a partner. We sell outcomes.
- ❌ **Custom branding work** — we partner with brand studios when needed, we don't build logos ourselves.
- ❌ **Hardware** (laser machines, etc.) — out of scope.
- ❌ **Medical training** — out of scope.

## Per-honey playbook structure (template for the 6 files)

Each honey gets its own playbook at `docs/skills/[01-06]-[honey-name].md` with:

1. **Definition** — what we deliver, in 1 paragraph
2. **Pricing** — SAR amount + rev-share if any
3. **Duration** — fixed days or ongoing
4. **Pre-requirements** — what we need from the clinic before starting
5. **Day-by-day execution** — specific calendar
6. **AI orchestration map** — which AIs do what (Claude, Gemini, Nano Banana Pro, ElevenLabs)
7. **Compliance hard lines** — PDPL/SFDA/SAMA constraints specific to this honey
8. **Sales pitch template** — exact 1-paragraph framing to use with the clinic owner
9. **Conversion mechanism** — how this honey leads to the next tier (Eid Pack → Sprint, etc.)
10. **Real case study slots** — 1-3 placeholder slots to fill after first delivery
