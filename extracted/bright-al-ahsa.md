- **Hashtag system:** #عيادات_برايت_التخصصية #تجميل #الاحساء #فيلر — clinic-led, treatment-led, city-led
- **Photographic style:** mixed — clinical (filler procedures, doctor mid-treatment) + lifestyle (sheep imagery for Eid) + product flat lays (price grids)

**Snapchat is their most-active channel** ([@bright-clinics](https://www.snapchat.com/@bright-clinics) — daily/weekly content May 2026). Snapchat content shows doctor-led short-form which IS effective; the Meta ad creative is the gap.

**Engagement gap:** 3,478 IG followers vs Almoosa 16,000 vs Kayan 8,426 → they pay agency to reach an audience their competitors built organically.

### 6.3 Agency footprint

- **Agency identity:** NOT NAMED publicly (no watermark, no IG bio credit, no LinkedIn "powered by")
